This is a Christmas interactive project. By the time I'm done, you'll be able to make your own Christmas Tree! In order for a good result, please go in order of the list of buttons. If your color name is two words, such as "light blue", remove the space.

  • This button puts a star on top of the tree!
  • This button puts a minion on top of the tree!
  • This button puts a frog on top of the tree!
  • Click to add ornaments!
  • This button will put presents under the tree.
  • This button places frog ornaments on the tree!

Note: Music plays when you click on the canvas. Only click on the canvas once! The music takes a about 5 seconds to load. If you click multiple times, the music will start every time you click.
